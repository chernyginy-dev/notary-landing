About the Notary

Private Notary

Private notary of the Kharkiv Notarial District with over 30 years of practice. Thousands of contracts, inheritance cases and notarial documents of varying complexity have been completed over this time.

The core focus is on real estate transactions, inheritance matters, powers of attorney and legally complex cases that demand experience and meticulous attention to detail.

The notary office is located in the very centre of Kharkiv — Rymarska St. 19 — providing easy access for all clients.

I guarantee legality, confidentiality and an individual approach for every client.
